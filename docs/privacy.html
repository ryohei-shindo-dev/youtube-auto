1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how gachiho-motive ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application. The App is designed to publish short-form investment motivation videos to social media platforms (including TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and X) on behalf of authenticated users.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Authentication Data: When you authorize the App through TikTok or other platforms, we receive and store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens. These tokens allow the App to post content on your behalf.
  • User Identifiers: We store your platform-specific user ID (e.g., TikTok open_id) solely for the purpose of identifying your account during content publishing.
  • Video Content: The App generates and uploads video content that you have authorized for publication.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your browsing history or location data
  • Your followers, following lists, or social connections
  • Your private messages or direct messages
  • Any financial or payment information

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate with social media platforms on your behalf
  • To upload and publish video content to your authorized accounts
  • To refresh expired authentication tokens to maintain service continuity

4. Data Storage and Security

All authentication tokens and credentials are stored locally on the developer's secure machine. We do not store any data on external servers or cloud databases. Access tokens are encrypted at rest and are never transmitted to any third party.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only transmitted to the respective social media platform APIs (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, X) for the sole purpose of content publishing as authorized by you.

6. Data Retention

We retain your authentication tokens only for as long as necessary to provide our services. Tokens are automatically invalidated when you revoke access through your platform settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access: You can revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok settings (Settings > Security > Manage app permissions) or equivalent settings on other platforms.
  • Request data deletion: You can request deletion of all stored data by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete all tokens and identifiers associated with your account.
  • Access your data: You can request a copy of all data we store about you.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
