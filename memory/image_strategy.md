# 背景画像戦略

> 2026-03-08 確定

## 方針

- **長尺**: AI生成を主軸。基幹ビジュアル5枚を固定して繰り返し使う
- **Shorts**: AI生成中心 + 足りない分だけフリー素材（Pexels / Unsplash）
- 混在OK。長尺だけはAI生成で統一感を保つ

## 素材ポリシー

- 人物は小さく、顔は見せすぎない
- チャートやアプリ画面は主役にしない
- 札束、高級車、成功者演出は使わない
- 色調・余白・人物サイズを固定して統一感を出す

## ライセンス注意

- Pexels: 商用利用可、改変可、クレジット不要。推薦誤認・無加工再販・再配布は不可
- OpenAI画像生成: 出力は利用者が所有。ただし同種出力が他ユーザーにも生成されうる（非独占）
- Midjourney: 商用利用はプランと規約による。使うなら都度確認

## 長尺用・基幹ビジュアル5枚

生成サイズ: **2880x1620 以上**（16:9、scale→crop で1.5倍拡大するため）
色調: deep navy / charcoal / muted brown 系で統一

### 1. 夜の思索（hook / empathy 向き、zoom in）

**フリー素材検索語**
man sitting alone dark room window moody / person thinking at night dim room cinematic / solitary figure room blue light moody

**AI生成プロンプト**
A horizontal 16:9 cinematic background, a solitary person sitting quietly in a dim room at night, seen from behind or distant side angle, face mostly hidden, soft window light or a faint desk lamp, deep navy and charcoal palette, large negative space for text, quiet contemplative mood, realistic painterly, subtle haze, restrained film still, no trading screens, no luxury, no drama

### 2. 扉の光（resolve / closing 向き、zoom out）

**フリー素材検索語**
dark corridor open door light moody / slightly open door cinematic shadow / door light beam dark hallway

**AI生成プロンプト**
A horizontal 16:9 cinematic background, a heavy old door slightly open in a dark quiet corridor, soft warm light gently spilling through the narrow gap into a blue-gray space, off-center composition, large negative space for text, minimal composition, quiet moody atmosphere, realistic painterly, subtle haze, restrained and elegant, no fantasy, no religion

### 3. 灯りのある部屋（action 向き、zoom out）

**フリー素材検索語**
dim room lamp chair moody minimal / quiet room warm lamp cinematic / empty room desk chair soft light dark

**AI生成プロンプト**
A horizontal 16:9 cinematic background, a dim simple room with a small warm lamp, plain desk and chair, deep blue-gray and muted brown tones, large negative space for text, minimal uncluttered composition, calm safe restrained atmosphere, realistic painterly, subtle haze, no cozy influencer style, no charts, no luxury

### 4. 待つ人（interpret 向き、zoom out）

**フリー素材検索語**
person sitting alone bench from behind moody / solitary figure sitting dark space cinematic / quiet waiting person back view minimal

**AI生成プロンプト**
A horizontal 16:9 cinematic background, a small solitary person sitting quietly on a simple bench or chair, seen from behind or far away, face not visible, looking into a wide dark space, deep navy and charcoal tones, subtle warm distant light, large negative space for text, quiet contemplative mood, realistic painterly, subtle haze, no heroism, no success symbolism

### 5. 夜明け前の道（closing 向き、zoom out）

**フリー素材検索語**
road before dawn moody empty path / quiet path early dawn cinematic / dark road horizon light minimal

**AI生成プロンプト**
A horizontal 16:9 cinematic background, a long quiet path before dawn stretching into the distance, faint pale light on the horizon, mostly dark blue-gray scene, very small distant figure optional, large negative space for text, calm reflective understated hope, realistic painterly, subtle haze, minimal landscape, not epic, not adventurous
